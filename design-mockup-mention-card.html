What changed and why
  • Issue chip — key bold, title truncated with ellipsis, status pill inside chip. Title no longer floods the row.
  • @mention chips — blue background (#E9F2FE) + avatar initial + blue text. Immediately scannable in the body.
  • CC chips — neutral style (grey, not blue) to distinguish CC from direct mention. Labeled "CC" in uppercase 11px.

  • Footer zone — border-top groups reactions + reply + composer. Reply ghost btn right-aligned for fast action.
  • Ask Caty — moved inline with the composer (same row), shorter label. Removes the orphaned line below the textarea.
