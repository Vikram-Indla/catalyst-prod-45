---
name: promote-decision
description: Convert an explicitly approved exploration conclusion into a durable decision and requirement delta.
---

Only run after the user explicitly says the decision is locked or approved.

1. Resolve project and feature.
2. Identify the exact approved delta.
3. Create a decision ID.
4. Record evidence, rationale, alternatives, consequences, and superseded assumptions.
5. Update requirement deltas.
6. Create candidate feature packets.
7. Never mark a packet implementation-ready without technical discovery and testable acceptance criteria.
